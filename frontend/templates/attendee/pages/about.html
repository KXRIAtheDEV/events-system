About EventHub

Kenya's premier event discovery and ticketing platform

Our Story

EventHub was founded in 2020 with a simple mission: to make event discovery and ticketing seamless for everyone in Kenya. We saw a gap in the market where event organizers struggled to sell tickets online, and attendees found it difficult to discover exciting events happening around them.

Today, EventHub has grown to become Kenya's leading event ticketing platform, hosting thousands of events and serving millions of happy attendees. From intimate workshops to large-scale music festivals, we're proud to power experiences that bring people together.

Our Mission

To connect people with unforgettable experiences through a seamless, secure, and intuitive event discovery and ticketing platform.

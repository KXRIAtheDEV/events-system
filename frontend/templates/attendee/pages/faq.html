Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about EventHub

How do I book tickets for an event?
Simply browse events, select your preferred ticket type, choose quantity, and complete the payment. You'll receive a QR code ticket via email and SMS.
How do I become an organizer?
Click "Become an Organizer" on the homepage, register with your organization details, submit required documents, and start creating events after verification.
What payment methods are accepted?
We accept M-Pesa, Credit/Debit cards (Visa, Mastercard), and PayPal. All payments are secure and encrypted.
How do I get a refund?
Contact our support team within 7 days of purchase. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days to your original payment method.
How do I contact customer support?
Email us at support@eventhub.co.ke, call +254 700 000000, or use the contact form on our website. We respond within 24 hours.
Can I transfer my ticket to someone else?
Yes, you can transfer tickets through your account dashboard. The recipient will receive an email to accept the transfer.
How do I find my tickets after purchase?
Log into your account and go to "My Tickets". You can view, download PDF, or access QR codes for all your purchased tickets.
Is my payment information secure?
Yes, we use industry-standard SSL encryption and never store your full payment details on our servers.

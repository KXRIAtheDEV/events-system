Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book tickets?
Simply browse events, select your preferred event, choose ticket quantity, and proceed to checkout. You'll receive a confirmation email with your tickets.
How do I get my tickets?
After successful payment, you'll receive a QR code via email and in your account dashboard. Simply show the QR code at the venue for entry.
Can I get a refund?
Refund policies vary by event. Please check the event details page or contact the event organizer directly for their refund policy.
What payment methods are accepted?
We accept M-Pesa, Visa, Mastercard, and other major credit cards. All payments are processed securely.
How do I reset my password?
Click on "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your email, and we'll send you a link to reset your password.
How do I update my profile?
Go to your Profile page from the Account dropdown menu, then click Edit to update your personal information.
How do I contact the organizer?
Each event page has organizer contact information. You can also reach out to our support team for assistance.
